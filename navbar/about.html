About us

Learn about us

Founded in 2018 by two lifelong friends and coffee enthusiasts, Brew Haven was born out of a shared dream: to create a space where coffee is more than a beverage—it’s a ritual, a conversation, a culture. Our beans are sourced directly from smallholder farms across Kenya, Ethiopia, and Rwanda, ensuring ethical practices and exceptional quality.


We believe in slow mornings, bold brews, and honest hospitality. Our team of baristas is trained not just in technique, but in storytelling—because every blend has a journey, and every customer deserves to be part of it.


At Brew Haven, we don’t just serve coffee. We serve moments.
